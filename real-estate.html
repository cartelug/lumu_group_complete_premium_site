How buying works

Verified before you pay.

Property in Uganda rewards caution. Before a single shilling moves, we check three things — on every deal, no exceptions.

Title checked

A search at the land registry confirms the title is genuine, current and in the seller’s name — not a photocopy and a promise.

Ground verified

We walk the actual plot with you, confirm the boundaries and talk to the neighbours — so what’s on paper matches what’s on the ground.

Paper trail

Sale agreements, transfer documents and receipts done properly, with copies in your hands — so the deal survives any question later.
