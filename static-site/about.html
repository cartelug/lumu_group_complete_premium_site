The story

A workshop with a rule.

Lumu opened its gate in 2018 with one founding idea: car owners in Kampala deserve a garage they don't have to second-guess.

So every job here starts with a written quote and ends with work we're willing to stand behind. That rule built the workshop — and the workshop grew from repairs into genuine parts, computerized diagnostics, fleet logistics and a real-estate division. Different jobs, different customers, same rule everywhere.

What we stand for

Four things we don't trade.

Whether it's a warning light, a fleet contract or a land title, the same four things hold on every Lumu job card.

Honesty

A written quote before any spanner turns. We don't invent faults and we don't pad the bill.

Quality

Genuine, brand-matched parts and work done properly — not patched to survive the week.

Speed

Fast diagnosis and efficient repairs, because a parked car costs you money every day it sits.

Relationships

We build for the customer who comes back for years — that's the whole business, not a slogan.
